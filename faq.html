Frequently asked questions

All the details about the stream, schedule, how to join, rules, and setup - in one place.

When do you stream?

Most weeks we go live Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 6:00 PM – 10:00 PM, with occasional bonus Saturday streams. Follow on Twitch for notifications.

What games do you play?

Currently VALORANT and Supervive are the mains. We sprinkle in variety depending on mood and community votes.

How do I join community games?

Join the Discord for party-up channels and ping roles: discord.gg/DuN3RvmY6m. We host viewer lobbies on stream - watch for the callouts.

What are the stream rules?
  • Be kind - zero tolerance for harassment or hate.
  • No backseat gaming unless asked.
  • Avoid spoilers.
  • Keep it TOS-friendly.
What’s your setup?

PC specs, peripherals, mic/cam details can be found on stream with !pc and !setup. I can add a full setup page if you want more detail here.

How do I get notifications?

Follow on Twitch and enable notifications. For extra reliability, join the Discord - we post a go‑live ping every stream.

How can I support the stream?

Hang out, chat, share clips - that’s huge. You can also sub on Twitch or subscribe on YouTube. Thanks for the support!
